# Differentiation

HELPER is differentiated less by a single model claim and more by product shape.

## What It Does Differently

- local-first execution instead of cloud-only reliance
- operator-guided workflows instead of opaque autonomous behavior
- structured runtime visibility instead of hidden state
- generated desktop artifacts instead of only chat responses
- public/private boundary honesty instead of overstated claims

## Why That Matters

For serious users and potential partners, controllability and traceability matter as much as raw capability. HELPER is designed to support those requirements at the product boundary, not just in the backend.

## What It Does Not Claim

- human-level parity today
- a completed authoritative blind evaluation today
- a completed counted parity window today
- universal superiority over every alternative
