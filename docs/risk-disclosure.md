# Risk Disclosure

This repository is intentionally honest about its limits.

## Material Risks

- human-level parity is not proven
- the public showcase is not the full runnable product
- private core implementation is excluded
- some screenshots can become stale as the UI evolves
- runtime data, logs, and internal evidence remain separate
- external dependencies and browser/API behavior may change

## What To Avoid Claiming

- do not claim completed parity
- do not claim the public showcase repo is the production core
- do not imply all internal evidence is public
- do not imply a turnkey SaaS deployment from the repository alone

## Why Publish This

Clear risk disclosure improves trust and makes the project more credible for technical reviewers, partners, and potential acquirers.
