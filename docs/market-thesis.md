# Market Thesis

There is strong demand for AI systems that can work inside private environments, preserve operator control, expose runtime state, and produce reviewable outputs.

## Likely Buyer / User Segments

- internal tooling teams
- AI infrastructure teams
- regulated organizations
- domain-specific software vendors
- systems integrators
- strategic acquirers looking for a controllable product shell

## Why The Market Exists

- organizations want private or edge-friendly AI workflows
- knowledge workers need structured planning and generation, not just chat
- teams increasingly care about evidence, auditability, and ownership
- reusable operator surfaces are more valuable when they can be adapted to real workflows

## Thesis

HELPER is positioned as a controllable execution shell for research, planning, generation, and runtime-guided workflows. That makes it relevant to product teams that need more than a prompt box and less than a full enterprise platform.
