# Business Model

This repository is a public showcase, so it does not encode a final monetization plan. The likely business paths are still worth documenting.

## Plausible Revenue Paths

- enterprise licensing for private deployments
- implementation and integration services
- support and maintenance agreements
- customization for specific domain workflows
- strategic acquisition of the product and its IP

## Why These Paths Fit

HELPER is shaped like a product shell and runtime platform, which means value can come from software ownership, deployment, support, adaptation, and technical diligence readiness rather than from a public open-source flywheel alone.

## What Is Not Claimed

- no public SaaS revenue claim
- no current subscription claim
- no claim that the public showcase repo alone is the monetization asset
